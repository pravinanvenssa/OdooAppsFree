Shopify Odoo Integration Overview

The Shopify Odoo Integration module provides a seamless connection between your Shopify store and Odoo platform. With this module, you can efficiently manage your Shopify products, orders, and customers directly within Odoo. Here's how it works:

  • Products: All Shopify products are synchronized and displayed under the "Products" section in Odoo, ensuring accurate inventory and data management.
  • Orders: Shopify orders are automatically synced into the "Orders" section of Odoo, allowing for efficient tracking and processing.
  • Customers: Shopify customers are integrated into the "Customers" section, providing a unified view of your customer base.

Key Benefits

  • Centralized Management: Manage all Shopify operations from the Odoo dashboard.
  • Enhanced Productivity: Eliminate manual data entry with automated synchronization.
  • Real-Time Updates: Ensure your data is always accurate and up-to-date.
  • Custom Reporting: Generate detailed reports for better decision-making.
  • Scalability: Easily scale your business with seamless integration.

Step-by-Step Setup

Follow these simple steps to set up the Shopify-Odoo integration:

  1. Install the Connector: Download and install the Shopify-Odoo Connector from the Odoo apps marketplace.
  2. Connect Shopify Account: In Odoo, connect your Shopify store by entering the necessary API credentials.
  3. Configure Product Sync: Set up synchronization rules for products, ensuring they are properly categorized in both platforms.
  4. Enable Order Sync: Activate automatic order syncing between Shopify and Odoo for real-time updates on customer orders.
  5. Customer Sync: Sync your Shopify customer base with Odoo for seamless CRM management.
